About the App:
CareSetu is a digital healthcare application that connects users to healthcare service providers
including clinics, hospitals, labs, and pharmacies for services such as consultations,
diagnostics, medicine delivery, and care planning.
User Roles:
- Primary User: Adult, account holder.
- Secondary User: Added by Primary User.
- HSP Registered User: Linked with a healthcare provider, assigned UHID/UMR.
- Non-Registered User: Limited access, no service bookings.
Privacy Policy Highlights:
- Data encrypted and stored on secure servers.
- Information accessed only with consent or during service/support.
- Complies with Indian data privacy regulations.
