CareSetu Application - Procedures and Workflows
1. Registration Process
- Download and install the CareSetu App.
- Register as a Primary User (adult, 18+).
- Add Secondary Users if needed.
- HSP Registered Users are assigned a UHID/UMR.
2. Booking Appointments
- Log in using your credentials.
- Choose service: consultation, diagnostics, medicine delivery, etc.
- Select a Healthcare Service Provider (HSP).
- Confirm appointment and make payment if necessary.
3. Uploading Prescriptions
- Upload a clear, valid prescription.
- Wait for verification by HSP.
- HSP will confirm or cancel based on compliance.
4. Online Consultation Process
- Choose your preferred consultation mode (video/call/chat).
- Provide medical history or take the self-assessment.
- Complete session and receive prescription or diagnostic advice.
5. Data Access and Security
- All medical data stored on secure cloud services.
- View and manage your health data through the app dashboard.
- ASPL may review anonymized records for quality purposes.
